TrackPulse Vic provides useful information about trains and public transport in Victoria, Australia

Here are just some of the features:

Log your trips

Log which trains, trams and busses you have been on, and view statistics about your travel habits. You can also see a map of where you've been.

Find where any Metro train is going

You can search for a specific train to see its current location and run as well as showing information about it and a photo if available.

Play a game

There are multiple games for you to play, sticking with the theme of "Victorian Trains". The most popular one is our station guesser game.
